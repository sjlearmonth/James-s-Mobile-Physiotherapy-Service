Physiotherapy Services


I offer physiotherapy services to my clients for the following conditions;


  • Sport injuries
  • Joint, muscle and nerve pain
  • Back and neck pain
  • Rehabilatation
  • Massage and manipulations
  • Elderly mobility and balance issues

I have my own transport so I can visit your home or place of residence to provide physiotherapy services. I am able to visit those living mainly in the following towns and villages;


  • Woburn
  • Woburn Sands
  • Leighton Buzzard
  • Milton Keynes
  • and other surrounding villages

Pricing for my services is as detailed below;


  • £60 for an one-hour initial assessment or treatment session.
  • £40 for a 30-minute follow-up

Please note that the following additional charges apply to cover transport costs;


  • £10 for travel within a 10-15 mile radius of Woburn
  • £20 for travel within a 15-20 mile radius of woburn
