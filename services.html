Physiotherapy Services


What is physiotherapy?


Physiotherapy is a conservative treatment aimed at people who have suffered an injury or disability, helping them to restore movement and function, and reduce symptoms of stiffness and pain. I take a whole body and systems approach when assessing musculoskeletal conditions and the initial assessment will help to identify how your symptoms are affecting you. I like to ensure that you are involved in the decision making throughout your physiotherapy journey, so treatments will be tailored specifically to you and your goals. Treatments may include education and advice, exercise prescription and hands on manual therapy such as soft tissue massage and manipulations.


I offer physiotherapy services to my clients for the following conditions;


  • Sport injuries and rehabilitation
  • Joint, muscle and nerve pain
  • Back pain and sciatica
  • Neck pain and whiplash
  • Thoracic pain
  • Post operative joint replacements and surgeries
  • Post fractures
  • Elderly mobility and balance issues
