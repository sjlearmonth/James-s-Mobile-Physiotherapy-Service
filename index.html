Welcome to James's Mobile Physiotherapy Service



Education and Training

Hello and welcome to my website. My name is James and I am a fully-qualified musculo-skeletal (MSK) physiotherapist and I live in Woburn, Bedfordshire. I graduated with a first class Bachelor of Science honours degree in Physiotherapy at Oxford Brookes University, Oxford in 2013.


Since qualifying as a physiotherapist, I have worked in Primary and Secondary musculo-skeletal healthcare services at Milton Keynes University Hospital and later at a GP's surgery. I have also worked as a sports physiotherapist at Buckingham Rugby Club for 3 years.


This training and work experience has involved me working with patients from a variety of backgrounds including sport injuries, post-operative joint replacements and surgeries, rheumatology patients, patients suffering with chronic pain conditions, such as fibromyalgia, and elderly patients with balance issues.



Physiotherapy Services

I specialise in providing physiotherapy services for the following conditions :-

  • Back pain
  • Sport injuries
  • Joint pain
  • Massage and manipulations for muscle and joint issues
  • Elderly mobility and balancing issues

I have my own transport so I am able to visit your home or place of work to provide my services.
